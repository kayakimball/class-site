My Neighbor Totoro

Released 1988

Two sisters, Satsuki and Mei, move to the Japanese countryside with their father. They befriend magical forest spirits, including the large, furry Totoro, while their mother is hospitalized. The climax occurs when Mei runs away to visit her mother, and Satsuki, with Totoro's help, finds her, leading to the sisters being reunited and learning their mother is recovering well.
